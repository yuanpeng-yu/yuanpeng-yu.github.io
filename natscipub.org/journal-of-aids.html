About This Journal

American Journal of AIDS (AJA) is an open access, peer-reviewed journal that considers articles on all aspects of AJA, the goal of this journal is to provide a platform and global forum for scientists and academicians all over the world to promote, share, and discuss various new issues and developments in different areas of AJA. This journal targets researchers, scientists, and students who seek to advance the knowledge related to its scope.

All submitted manuscripts must be in English and will be processed to a rigorous and fair peer-review. Accepted papers will immediately publish online. The original papers should include but are not limited to the following subject areas of AJA :

  •   AIDS and behavioral studies
  •   Diagnosis of HIV/AIDS
  •   Epidemiology of HIV/AIDS
  •   Historical significance of immunological studies and treatment of AIDS
  •   HIV/AIDS and clinical studies
  •   Infectious pathogens
  •   Novel findings on virology & immunology
  •   Opportunistic infections in HIV/AIDS
  •   Pathogenicity of HIV/AIDS
  •   Political Economy of HIV Infection/AIDS
  •   Prevention of HIV/AIDS
  •   Public awareness and prevention of HIV/AIDS
  •   Sexually transmitted diseases
  •   Treatment of HIV/AIDS
  •   Virology & immunology of HIV/AIDS

The journal welcomes both applied and basic Research Articles, Reviews, Letters to the Editor, Meeting Reports, Methodology Articles, Short reports, Notes, Study Protocols and advertisements.
