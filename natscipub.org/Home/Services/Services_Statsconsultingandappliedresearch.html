Statistical Consulting and Applied Research

ASP also provides advanced statistical consulting and cutting-edge research support for planning your study, developing the statistical models and analyzing the data.

We consult with university researchers and administrative units, private and public companies and government organizations. We can provide targeted statistical methods development as well as established, best-practice analysis.

For individual researchers we provide advice and collaboration for the statistical components of grant proposals including power analysis, identification of appropriate statistical methodology, data management and statistical report writing.

For companies we take contracts and provide solutions to business obstacles and resources to manipulate huge data sets. Our ability and skills in statistical methodology and communication makes our services valuable to clients ranging from Government entities, mid-sized businesses to research institutes.

  • Biomedical data analysis, public health and epidemiology, Carle foundation
  • Business and marketing, law
  • Bus system project
  • Civil and environmental engineering, agricultural and biological engineering, computer science
  • Climate change and environmental studies
  • Crop science, agriculture and animal science
  • Detect cheating for multiple choices of questions for testing
  • Detect fraudulent trading

  • Medical & NIH funding projects and studies: Alzheimer’s disease, Cardiovascular diseases, ECGs, MRI, Endocrine, Gastrointestinal, Hematology and oncology, Neurology, Psychiatry, Renal,Reproductive, Respiratory
  • Social work, sociology, history, anthropology, library science
  • Sports and recreation, kinesiology and community health, nutritional sciences

Consulting Rates

ASP provides fee-based statistical consulting services to members of the university community, government and industry.

Collaboration with ASP can be highly beneficial to your research program. We consult on projects ranging from a few hours of work to long term applied research.

ASP requires a deposit based on the estimated number of hours ASP will need to finish the project.

  • PhD Student Consultant: $80/hr
  • Manager Consultant: $150/hr
  • Director Consultant: $150/hr
  • Code release fee: $100

Contact

Please send your inquiry by email to statistical”at”natscipub.org and place the term "statistical" in the heading. The inquiry should be clearly state your proposal what kind of statistical services you would need.

A further discussion with you by replied email or a price will be quoted to you shortly after submission of the inquiry.
