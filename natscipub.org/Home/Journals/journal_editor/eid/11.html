British University in Egypt

Assistant Professor - Program Director

Chemical Engineering


Email:  elazabha@vcu.edu

Qualifications

  • 2018-2018 Doctor of Philosophy, Chemical Engineering. Virginia Commonwealth University (VCU), Richmond, VA, USA.
  • 2018-2018 Blended Learning Essentials. University of Leeds, UK. Master of Science, Chemical Engineering. M.T.C, Cairo, Egypt.
  • 2018-2018 Bachelor of Science, Chemical Engineering. M.T.C, Cairo, Egypt.
  • 2017-2018 Assistant Professor - Program Director, 09/2017 – Department of Chemical Engineering, British University in Egypt, BUE, Cairo, Egypt.
  • 2016-2018 Assistant Professor - Faculty Exam officer, 09/2016 – Department of Chemical Engineering, British University in Egypt, BUE, Cairo, Egypt.
  • 2015-2018 Assistant Professor - Department Exam Officer, 09/2015 – Department of Chemical Engineering, British University in Egypt, BUE, Cairo, Egypt.
  • 2014-2015 Assistant Professor, Researcher, 01/2014 – 08/2015 Department of Chemical Engineering, M.T.C, Cairo, Egypt.
  • 2014-2015 Assistant Professor, 01/2014 – 08/2015 Department of Chemical Engineering, M.T.C, Cairo, Egypt.
