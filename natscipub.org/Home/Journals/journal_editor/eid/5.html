Biography

Dr.   Tushar Borkar

Cleveland State University

Assistant Professor

Mechanical Engineering


Email:  t.borkar@csuohio.edu

https://www.csuohio.edu/engineering/mce/tushar-borkar

Qualifications

  • 2015-2018 Assistant Professor, Mechanical Engineering, Cleveland State University, Cleveland, OH
  • 2014-2015 Senior Research Scientist, University of North Texas, Denton, TX
  • 2013-2014 Postdoctoral Research Associate Adviser: Professor Rajarshi Banerjee, University of North Texas, Denton, TX
  • 2010-2013 Ph.D., Materials Science and Engineering, University of North Texas, Denton, TX
  • 2007-2010 MS, Mechanical and Aerospace Engineering, Oklahoma State University, Stillwater, OK
  • 2007-2007 B. Tech, Mechanical Engineering, Mumbai University, Mumbai, India
